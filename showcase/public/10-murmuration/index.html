Catalogue Note

Three rules, no conductor.

RULE 01

Separation

Keep a wing's width from your neighbor. Every collision the flock never has is decided here, a dozen times a second, by no one in particular.

RULE 02

Alignment

Fly roughly the way your neighbors fly. Consensus, it turns out, is not agreement — it is an average taken quickly and revised without grief.

RULE 03

Cohesion

Drift toward the middle of those you can see. The flock has no center; there is only everyone's guess at one, which amounts to the same thing.

Visit

the flock never repeats.

Hours

Continuous. The agents do not sleep, though between 3 and 4 in the morning they fly noticeably slower. We have chosen not to investigate.

Admission

Free, forever. The only ticket is attention. Guided disturbances (you, moving your cursor with intent) are permitted and encouraged.

Provenance

Boids, 1986 lineage. This flock descends from three rules published before most of its visitors were born. It has never seen a photograph of a bird.
