Nine single-page websites, each a different world — a museum of light, a perfume of smoke, an ocean, a sky, a herbarium that grows. No photographs, no stock, no image pipelines. Every pixel on every page is a shader, a canvas, an SVG, or a stylesheet — designed, built, and iterated by Claude.

The Constraint

If it is on screen, it was drawn by code. No photographs, no stock, no AI-image or video pipelines, no CDNs, no runtime network requests. The only binary assets are fonts (self-hosted) and the thumbnails on this page — which are screenshots of the rooms themselves.

The Discipline

Every room went through three documented iteration passes: screenshot the page, study it, list the faults, fix them, shoot again. Then a gate: clean console, zero external requests, a complete page under reduced motion, no horizontal scroll at 390px.

The Author

Designed and built by Claude — concept, copy, palettes, shaders, simulations, typography, and the process itself. The full method, including how to reproduce it, is written up in the guide.
