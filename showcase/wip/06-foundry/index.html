01 — MANIFESTO / READ ALOUD, PREFERABLY SHOUTED

Most typefaces ask politely for attention. GRIND VAR takes it — by the collar, in nine hundred weights.

Subtlety is a luxury of people with nothing to say. We built one axis for weight, one for width, and threw away the brakes.

It is licensed for posters, protests, price tags, and the funerals of lesser typefaces. It kerns by force. It hints at nothing.
